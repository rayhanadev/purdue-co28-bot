  • I submitted the form but I have not recieved an email. What do I do?

    Check your Spam folder first, the verification email may end up there. Also make sure you are using the Outlook option for your @purdue.edu account, sometimes email forwarding doesn't work.

    We use your @purdue.edu email address to ensure you are a Purdue student and protect our users!
